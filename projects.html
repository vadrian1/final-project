Projects

  • Hovercraft

    • Collaborated with a team to create a radio-controlled hovercraft that could complete a designated 60 feet figure 8 course in 51.8 seconds with 250 g of cargo
    • Modeled and prototyped 3D shells to find the optimal shape of a hovercraft using CATIA and windflow simulations, and physically produced the prototypes with a laser cutter.
    • Wired and coded and electrical system with an arduino, capable of reading in radio signals from an RC controller to operate the hovercraft.
    • Communicated finding and results through investor-style presentations and technical reports.

    • Exercised knowledge of data abstraction, creating ADT’s for players, decks, and cards to program a playable version of Euchre with options for multiple players
